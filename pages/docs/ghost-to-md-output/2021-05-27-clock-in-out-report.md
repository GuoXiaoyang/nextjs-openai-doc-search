# Clock in & out report

1. Go to **<Report>** on the side-menu
2. Click **<Reports>** tab on the top menu
3. Click **<Employees>** tab on the side-menu
4. Choose **<Clock in/out report>**
5. Choose the duration you want to know and click **<Run report>**
